In 2017, straight out of school, I spent a few months volunteering as the photographer for [Sunrise Cambodia](https://www.sunrisecambodia.org.au/) – a not-for-profit charity that helps at-risk and disadvantaged children reach their full potential.

During my time there, I got to see the incredible work they do providing a loving home, education and care for hundreds of orphaned, abandoned and at-risk children, as well as education for thousands of other children from poor communities.

As a fresh-faced 17 year old, it felt so special to contribute in the small way that I did and tell their story. 

Below is a small selection of photos from my time there, some used in their media publications, some not.
