Last week, I was honoured to be invited by SUEDE onto a panel with fellow designers and researchers doing great things at Google, Dovetail, Commbank, and KPMG. 

Some advice I shared:
- If you've redesigned your website 5 times this year, it may be time to think PDF.
- Everyone has been a junior. 
- Not everyone has been a junior with the amount of noise and unrealistic expectations that exist now. 
- No one is up to speed with everything. 
- Everyone is figuring it out as they go.
- Be genuine, confident, communicative, and curious. 
- Designers are there to have an opinion. Make it.

Lastly, being a creative professional ≠ being creative. 

Whilst they overlap, it's important now more than ever to keep the creative spark alive (maybe through an [[Inspiration garden|inspiration garden]]) outside a professional realm that announces 'design is dead' every second week.

Always nice to speak in person, hopefully I stifled some anxieties, kindled the creative spark, and make a few people laugh at the same time.

Thanks to Natasha for organising, as well as my fellow panelists (and uni friends) Maria, Lachlan, Christina and Nabil.
